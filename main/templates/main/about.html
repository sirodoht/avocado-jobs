About

Avocado Jobs is a simple tool made exclusively for software people.

Neither hiring nor choosing a company are easy tasks for software companies and software engineers respectively. It is common for candidates to apply to over 100 roles, thus keeping track of all this information is mandatory for sanity maintenance. Avocado Jobs does exactly that; it enables you to keep track of all the roles you have applied as a job seeking individual. Furthermore, it provides you with extra functionality to aid in the data manipulation.


Privacy statement

Data that is provided to Avocado Jobs by its users is transported exclusively via HTTP using strong encryption. The server of avocadojobs.com is located in New Jersey, US and the database is backed up on a daily basis in Oregon, US. Users' data will never be sold, donated, or become accessible by any third parties. Finally, Avocado Jobs does not track its users across the web.


Avocado Jobs is developed by Theodore Keloglou.
